# Identification-Of-Herbal-Plants
Both human life and other forms of life on the planet depend heavily on herbal plants. The largest impact on the natural cycle is also exerted by herbal remedies. In biology and agriculture, it is becoming more crucial to recognize herbal plants. As a result, we created an Android-based smartphone application for identifying herbal plants based on images of their leaves.
